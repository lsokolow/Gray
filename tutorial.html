How to play

To move the hero

Space to use hero's abilities

To move the villain character (T,F,G,H)

(X,Z) to use villain character abilities

There will be different scenarios and you will be given 3 choices. Depending on the scenarios the objective is to collect the items or people and defeat the enemy to move on to the next level.

You will have to collect your tockens and capture the enemy in order to win.
